## Sales Narrative

### Core Message

**"No Frozen Money. Rule-Based Escrow. Transparent Disputes."**

---

### Key Points

#### 1. "No Frozen Money"

**Message**: Funds are not frozen in escrow. They flow according to clear rules.

**Explanation**:
- Immediate release on delivery (85% of funds)
- Holdback released on approval (15% of funds)
- No arbitrary freezes
- Predictable cash flow

**Benefit**: Faster cash flow, better user experience

---

#### 2. "Rule-Based, Auditable Escrow"

**Message**: All settlements follow published rules, not manual decisions.

**Explanation**:
- Clear rules for every scenario
- Automated settlement when possible
- Full audit trail
- No hidden decisions

**Benefit**: Transparency, trust, predictability

---

#### 3. "Disputes Resolve Automatically or Transparently"

**Message**: Disputes resolve automatically (TTL) or through transparent admin review.

**Explanation**:
- Auto-approve if no issue raised (inspection TTL)
- Disputes auto-resolve if not resolved within TTL
- Admin review is rule-constrained (not arbitrary)
- Full transparency

**Benefit**: Faster resolution, fair outcomes, reduced disputes

---

### Differentiation

**vs. Traditional Escrow**:
- Traditional: Manual, slow, opaque
- Ours: Automated, fast, transparent

**vs. Payment Platforms**:
- Payment platforms: No escrow, disputes handled manually
- Ours: Rule-based escrow, automated dispute resolution

**vs. Legal Services**:
- Legal services: Expensive, slow, case-by-case
- Ours: Affordable, fast, rule-based

## 1-Page Pitch

### Trust & Escrow Platform — 1-Page Pitch

**Headline**: "No Frozen Money. Rule-Based Escrow. Transparent Disputes."

---

#### Problem

- Buyers and sellers struggle with trust in transactions
- Traditional escrow is slow, manual, and opaque
- Payment disputes are costly and time-consuming

---

#### Solution

**Trust & Escrow Platform** provides rule-based, auditable escrow services:

- **No Frozen Money**: Funds flow according to clear rules (85% on delivery, 15% on approval)
- **Rule-Based Settlement**: All outcomes follow published rules, not manual decisions
- **Transparent Disputes**: Disputes resolve automatically (TTL) or through transparent admin review

---

#### How It Works

1. **Deal Created**: Buyer and seller agree on terms
2. **Funds Deposited**: Buyer deposits funds into escrow
3. **Delivery Confirmed**: Seller confirms delivery, 85% released immediately
4. **Inspection Period**: 3-7 days for buyer to inspect
5. **Auto-Approval**: If no issue, deal auto-approves, 15% released
6. **Dispute Resolution**: If issue raised, rule-based resolution within TTL

---

#### Benefits

**For Buyers**:
- Protected payments
- Fast dispute resolution
- Transparent process

**For Sellers**:
- Faster cash flow (85% on delivery)
- Reduced disputes
- Predictable settlement

**For Partners**:
- Trust-building tool
- Reduced payment disputes
- Scalable solution

---

#### Pricing

**Per-Deal**: 1.5% of deal amount (charged after settlement)

## Sales Process

### Step 1: Prospecting

**Channels**:
- Direct outreach (email, phone)
- Trade shows
- Industry associations
- Referrals

**Qualification**:
- Deal volume (10+ deals/month)
- Pain points (trust, disputes)
- Budget (can afford subscription or per-deal fees)

---

### Step 2: Discovery

**Questions**:
- How many deals do you handle per month?
- What are your biggest pain points?
- How do you currently handle escrow/payments?
- What would make escrow better for you?

**Goals**:
- Understand pain points
- Identify fit
- Build rapport

---

### Step 3: Demo

**Demo Flow**:
1. Show deal creation
2. Show fund deposit
3. Show delivery confirmation (85% release)
4. Show inspection period (auto-approve)
5. Show dispute resolution (if applicable)
6. Show partner dashboard

**Key Points**:
- "No frozen money" - show immediate release
- "Rule-based" - show published rules
- "Transparent" - show full timeline

---

### Step 4: Proposal

**Proposal Includes**:
- Pricing (subscription or per-deal)
- Contract terms
- SLA
- Onboarding plan

**Follow-Up**:
- Answer questions
- Address concerns
- Negotiate terms (if needed)

---

### Step 5: Onboarding

**Onboarding Process**:
1. Sign contract
2. Set up partner account
3. Provide training
4. Create first deal (test)
5. Go live

**Support**:
- Dedicated onboarding manager
- Training materials
- Support during first deals
